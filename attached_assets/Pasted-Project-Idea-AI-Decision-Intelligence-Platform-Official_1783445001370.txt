Project Idea: AI Decision Intelligence Platform
Official Problem Statement

Managers require intelligent recommendations instead of raw reports to make better business decisions.

The Real-World Problem

Businesses generate large amounts of data every day:

Sales
Expenses
Inventory
Customer orders
Marketing campaigns
Employee performance
Website traffic

However, most business owners only see dashboards and reports.

The challenge is that reports tell you what happened, but not why it happened or what to do next.

For example:

Dashboard:

Sales ↓ 18%
Customer retention ↓ 12%
Inventory ↑ 30%

A manager still has to analyze:

Why did sales drop?
Which product caused it?
Is inventory overstocked?
What action should I take?

Many SMEs don't have dedicated data analysts, so valuable data goes unused.

Proposed Solution

An AI-powered Decision Intelligence platform that continuously analyzes business data and provides:

Business insights
Root cause analysis
Risk detection
Demand forecasting
AI-generated recommendations
Action plans

Instead of just showing charts, the system tells business owners what happened, why it happened, and what they should do next.
